# aerocement

Lightweight foamed concrete with alkali-resistant fiber, mixable with a drill and a bucket.

AR-GFRC aerocement: open-source foamed concrete targeting >=3 MPa compressive / >=1.5 MPa flexural at <=1000 kg/m3, load-matched to single-story walls. Failures are documented on purpose.

**Status:** prototype. Targets are not yet physically validated. Known errors are pinned and public on purpose -- see the issues tab.

## Build one (small test batch)

1. Read the bill of materials: https://github.com/jesseray718/aerocement/blob/main/BOM.md
2. Mix a 1-liter test batch; record your ratios and results.
3. Post a photo + ratios in Discussions. An independent test batch is the most valuable contribution this project can receive today.

## Safety

Cement is caustic: wear gloves and eye protection. Cut glass fiber irritates skin and lungs: wear a mask when handling dry fiber. Test batches only; no structural use until independently validated.
