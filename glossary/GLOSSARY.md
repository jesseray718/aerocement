# OpenRoot Glossary

- **AE-GFRC**: Aerated Glass-Fiber Reinforced Concrete — lightweight mix for non-structural jobs with self-core sampling validation.
- **PoPW**: Proof-of-Physical-Work — verifiable physical actions logged as work ledger entries.
- **ACRE**: Economic primitive tied to verified work, assets, and community yield.
- **50-node cooperative**: Decentralized optimum with rotating roles, equal profit/asset share, replaceability, shared farm, 501c3 feedback.
- **Fractal bots**: Per-node local AI inference + group coordination for safety and maximum systemic benefit.
- **Hydration**: Adding live context, backlinks, and RAG-ready structure to files.
- **Tasker loop**: Clipboard → floating widget → Termux (context) → Markor log → clean clipboard output.
- **Zero-history bootstrap**: Starting with no credit/bank history; using builders at 1% util while separating personal vs business tracks.
