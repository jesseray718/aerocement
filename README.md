## 🌍 The Mission
Aerocement is a **zero-electricity, passive HVAC and power generation system** designed to eliminate dependency on exploitative energy monopolies. It combines open-cell breathable cement, geothermal cooling, and solar thermal thermosiphons to provide heating, cooling, and mechanical power for homes, villages, and even Mars habitats.

## 🔬 How It Works
### 1. The Material: Aerocement
An open-cell, carbon-infused cement that allows air and water to flow through its capillary structure.
- **Formula:** Cement + Xanthan Gum + Isopropyl Alcohol + Dawn Ultra + Activated Carbon.
- **Key Feature:** Creates a massive surface area for heat exchange and acts as a blackbody absorber (98% solar absorption).
- **Status:** *Formulation finalized. Physical properties (porosity, strength) pending experimental validation.*

### 2. The System: Passive Loop
1. **Solar Thermal:** Modular 4x8 panels absorb heat, creating a strong thermosiphon (natural airflow).
2. **Desiccant:** Waste heat dries incoming air to near-zero humidity.
3. **Geothermal Cooling:** Dry air passes through a 10ft underground labyrinth of wet Aerocement, cooling to ~35°F (1.7°C).
4. **Power:** The temperature differential drives a Stirling engine for mechanical/electrical power.

### 3. The Math (Theoretical)
- **Cooling Output:** Up to 20°F below ambient (target 35°F).
- **Power Output:** ~1kW per 3-panel stack (scaling linearly).
- **Efficiency:** Zero electricity required.
