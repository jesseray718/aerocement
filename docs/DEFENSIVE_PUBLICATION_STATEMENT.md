**Project:** Aerocement Passive Solar-Thermal Powerplant

## 1. STATEMENT OF INTENT
This document serves as a formal **Defensive Publication** of the Aerocement technology. The purpose of this publication is to place the invention into the public domain as **Prior Art**, thereby preventing any third party (corporate or governmental) from obtaining a patent on the core concepts, formulations, or system architectures described herein.

## 2. SCOPE OF PRIOR ART
The following elements are hereby declared as publicly disclosed and constitute Prior Art as of the date above:
1. **Material Formulation**: The specific open-cell, carbon-infused cement mixture utilizing xanthan gum, isopropyl alcohol, Dawn Ultra surfactant, and activated carbon to create a breathable, capillary-active structure.
2. **System Architecture**: The integration of modular 4x8 blackbody solar thermal panels with a geothermal evaporative cooling labyrinth (10ft depth) driven by a passive thermosiphon.
3. **Thermodynamic Process**: The method of using waste heat to regenerate desiccant beds and the specific airflow path (Desiccant -> Geothermal Tunnel -> Conditioned Space -> Solar Chimney) to achieve sub-ambient cooling without electricity.
4. **Scaling Methodology**: The modular stacking and interconnection of panels to scale from single-home to megacity applications.
